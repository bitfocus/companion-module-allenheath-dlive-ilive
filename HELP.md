# Allen & Heath dLive & iLive module

Controls the Allen & Heath dLive and iLive Mixers.

## Functions:

- Mute Channel
- Mute FX Buses
- Mute Group _(dLive only)_
- Mute DCA
- Fader Levels
- Mute Assign
- DCA Assign
- Recall Scene
- Talkback On _(dLive only)_
- Virtual SoundCheck mode _(dLive only)_

> Current version: 1.3.5
